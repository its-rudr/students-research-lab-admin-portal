Place student images in this folder.

Supported auto-detect names:
1) <enrollment_no>.png|jpg|jpeg|webp
2) <student-name-slug>.png|jpg|jpeg|webp
   Example: "Aarav Mehta" -> aarav-mehta.jpg

You can also store a direct path or URL in students_details.photo_url.
Examples:
- /student-photos/2024CS001.jpg
- https://example.com/photos/2024CS001.jpg
